Problem Statement

Buea neighborhoods face irregular waste collection, poor recycling separation, overflowing bins and limited municipal visibility into hotspots.

How It Works

1. Users request pickup. 2. Collectors accept nearby jobs. 3. OTP/photo confirms completion. 4. Recyclables enter the marketplace. 5. EcoCoins reward participation.

Environmental Impact

Track landfill diversion, CO₂ reduction, recycling rates, jobs created and neighborhood-level collection trends.

FAQ

Can households earn rewards?

Yes. EcoCoins are awarded for recycling, successful pickups, consistency and referrals.

How are collectors verified?

Collector signup captures ID verification, vehicle information and collection license number.

Does it support smart bins?

Yes. Bins above 80% show alerts and can auto-generate pickup requests.
